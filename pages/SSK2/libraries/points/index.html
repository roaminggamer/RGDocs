    • h_head(...) - Treats points list like a queue/FIFO and pushes one or more point sets (in reverse order) onto the points list front. Like calling insert(1, x1,y1), insert(1, x2,y2), ..., insert(1, xN,yN),
    • peek_head() - Treats points list like a queue/FIFO and retrieves the point at the front of the points list. Like a get at 1.
    • pop_head(x,y) - Treats points list like a queue/FIFO and pops the point at the front off the points list. Like a remove at 1.
